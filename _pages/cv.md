## Profile

I am **Dr. Tariq Ali Arain**, a researcher in **deep learning, computer vision, robust representation learning, and algorithm optimization**. I received my Ph.D. in Software Engineering from Hohai University, China, in March 2026. My research focuses on person re-identification, transformer-based architectures, metric learning, cross-resolution learning, occlusion robustness, and domain generalization. I am also interested in multimodal AI, generative AI, medical image analysis, surgical video understanding, and efficient AI systems for real-world deployment.

## Work Experience

### AI Research Engineer  
**Hohai University**  
*Nanjing, China | Sep 2021 -- Mar 2026*

- Conducted research in **Person Re-Identification (ReID)** for real-world surveillance, focusing on low-resolution recognition, occlusion robustness, and cross-camera variation.
- Developed deep learning models including **SwinReID**, **FocusReID**, and **VarReID** using transformers, attention mechanisms, multi-scale fusion, and metric learning.
