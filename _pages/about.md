Welcome! I am Dr. Tariq Ali Arain, a deep learning and computer vision researcher. I received my Ph.D. in Software Engineering from Hohai University, China, in March 2026.

My research focuses on robust representation learning, person re-identification, transformer-based architectures, metric learning, cross-resolution learning, occlusion robustness, and domain generalization. I am interested in developing reliable, efficient, and interpretable AI systems for complex real-world environments.

## Research Interests

- Deep Learning
- Computer Vision
- Person Re-Identification
- Robust Representation Learning
- Transformer Architectures
- Metric Learning
- Domain Generalization
- Multimodal AI
- Efficient and Interpretable AI

## Education

- **Ph.D. in Software Engineering**, Hohai University, China, 2026

## Selected Research Directions

My current research interests include robust visual recognition under challenging conditions such as low resolution, occlusion, domain shift, and imperfect data. I am also interested in multimodal AI, vision-language learning, and efficient deep learning models for real-world deployment.
