Welcome! I am **Dr. Tariq Ali Arain**, a researcher in **deep learning, computer vision, intelligent visual systems, and algorithm optimization**. I received my Ph.D. in Software Engineering from **Hohai University, China**, in March 2026.

My research focuses on **robust representation learning**, **person re-identification**, **transformer-based architectures**, **metric learning**, **cross-resolution learning**, **occlusion robustness**, and **domain generalization**. I am broadly interested in developing reliable, efficient, and interpretable AI systems for complex real-world environments, including surveillance, healthcare, medical image analysis, surgical video understanding, and multimodal visual intelligence.

My earlier research also includes **algorithm design and optimization** for large-scale computing systems, with a focus on multi-objective optimization, scheduling, heuristic and meta-heuristic methods, and resource allocation in data centers.

## Selected Research Directions

My current research interests include robust visual recognition under challenging conditions such as low resolution, occlusion, cross-camera variation, domain shift, and imperfect visual data. I am also interested in multimodal AI, vision-language learning, generative AI, medical imaging, surgical video analysis, algorithm optimization, and efficient deep learning models for real-world deployment.

## Research Vision

My long-term research goal is to develop robust, efficient, and interpretable AI systems that can generalize across complex real-world environments. I aim to bridge computer vision, representation learning, multimodal intelligence, and optimization to design machine learning methods that are not only accurate, but also scalable, explainable, and suitable for practical deployment.
