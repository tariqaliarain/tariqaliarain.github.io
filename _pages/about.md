Welcome! I am Dr. Tariq Ali Arain, a researcher in deep learning, computer vision, and intelligent visual systems. I received my Ph.D. in Software Engineering from Hohai University, China, in March 2026.

My research focuses on robust representation learning, person re-identification, transformer-based architectures, metric learning, cross-resolution learning, occlusion robustness, and domain generalization. I am broadly interested in developing reliable, efficient, and interpretable AI systems for complex real-world environments, including surveillance, healthcare, medical image analysis, surgical video understanding, and multimodal visual intelligence.

## Research Interests

- Robust Representation Learning

- Multimodal and Generative AI

- Efficient and Interpretable AI

## Selected Research Directions

My current research interests include robust visual recognition under challenging conditions such as low resolution, occlusion, domain shift, and imperfect data. I am also interested in multimodal AI, vision-language learning, generative AI, medical imaging, surgical video analysis, and efficient deep learning models for real-world deployment.
